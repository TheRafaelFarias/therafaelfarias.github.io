ABOUT

I'm a Brazilian developer born on the 30th of December who has always loved creating things. At the age of 11, I discovered programming through the Minecraft game and over time I started configuring servers and then creating plugins for these servers until I discovered web development.

I went deeper and deeper learning Javascript and stumbled into the world of NodeJS, thus being able to create fullstack applications. I also developed bots for Discord and after that I became a master in Javascript for Web and NodeJS applications.

Specialized in React, React Native and NodeJS, today I can create applications from scratch using JS/TS with readable, optimized, fast codes and with lots of love and care
